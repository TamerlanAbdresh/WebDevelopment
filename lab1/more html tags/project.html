Caesar salad

  • Time: 10-15min
  • Serves: 1-2days
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken fillet 1pc.
White bread 200gr.
Quile eggs 2pcs.
Leaf lettuce 150gr.
Cherry tommatoes 200gr.
Hard cheese 150gr.

Step 1: First boil the chicken fillet.
Step 2:While the chicken is cooking, make the salad croutons.
Step 3:Hard boil quail eggs for salad.
Step 4:Wash the lettuce leaves and dry well, laying out on a paper towel.
Step 5:Cut the chicken fillet into small cubes. Wash cherry tomatoes, dry and cut into halves.
Step 6:Cut half of the cheese into cubes, the rest - grate on a fine grater.
Step 7:Make garlic sauce for a simple chicken Caesar salad.
Step 8:Lay half of the lettuce leaves on a flat plate and pour over them with a small amount of dressing. Then lay half of the crackers and chicken fillet. Pour sauce over again. Put the remaining lettuce leaves, croutons, chicken fillet and diced cheese.
Step 9:Drizzle with remaining garlic sauce. Garnish a simple Caesar salad with chicken with cherry tomatoes and quail eggs. Sprinkle with grated cheese and serve immediately.
